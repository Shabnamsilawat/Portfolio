MineStrone Soup

MineStrone Soup:

Minestrone is a hearty Italian vegetable soup made with traditionally leftover vegetables, tomato broth, herbs and often with addition of pasta or rice. There is no set recipe for minestrone, since it can be usually made out ofwhatever vegetables are available.

Ingredients:

  • Tomato:-200gms
  • Onion:-100gms
  • Zucchini:-100gms
  • Leek & Celery- 40gms
  • Carrot- 100gms
  • Potato-100gms
  • Beans-50gms
  • Garlic-30gms
  • Basil leaves-10gms
  • Pasta/Macaroni: 100gms
  • Parmesan cheese: 30gms
  • Vegetable stock: 1.5 ltr
  • Olive oil: 50ml
  • Oregano dry herbs: a pinch
  • Seasoning: as required

Method:

Chop garlic, leek, and celery. Cut all vegetables into small dices or brunoise shape. Sauté garlic, leek & celery in olive oil, add the vegetables and sauté 2–3 minutes. Add pasta and cook with vegetable stock until done. Adjust seasoning and garnish with basil and shaved parmesan. Drizzle with olive oil.
