Fettocine Carbonara

Fettocine Carbonara:

Fettuccine means "small ribbons", a pasta type similar to tagliatelle. Typically served with creamy sauces like Alfredo or Carbonara (bacon, cream, eggs, parsley, cheese, and black pepper).

Ingredients:

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Olive oil: 30–40ml
  • Butter: 20g
  • Bacon rashers (chopped): 175g
  • Garlic cloves (crushed): 2
  • Shallot/onion: 40gms
  • White wine: 20ml
  • Fresh parsley (chopped): 2 tsp
  • Egg yolks: 2
  • Heavy cream: ½ cup
  • Parmesan (grated): ⅓ cup

Method:

Chop bacon, garlic, shallots. Mix yolks with cream. Boil pasta till al dente. Sauté bacon, garlic, and shallot in oil, add wine, reduce, then add pasta. Stir in cream mixture, cook gently. Season and add parmesan and parsley. Serve hot.
